Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

My Story - Bedtime Tales ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address and password (hashed) when you create an account.

Child Profiles: First name, age group (4-6, 7-9, or 10-12), and avatar selection. We do NOT collect last names, photos, exact birth dates, or any other personally identifiable information about children.

Story Preferences: Theme, mood, duration, and optional moral lesson selections used to generate stories.

Usage Data: Anonymous analytics events (story generated, story read, rating given) to improve the service. No personal data is included in analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate personalized bedtime stories based on your preferences
  • To save your story library and reading progress
  • To process subscription payments (via Apple App Store)
  • To improve story quality based on anonymous ratings

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App is designed for use by parents and caregivers reading stories to children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do NOT collect personal information directly from children
  • Child profiles are created and managed by the parent/guardian
  • We only store the child's first name and age group — no photos, location, or contact information
  • Children cannot create accounts, make purchases, or communicate with others through the App
  • Generated stories are not shared publicly — they are private to the family's account

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
  • Communication between the App and our servers is encrypted
  • Story content is stored securely and associated with your account
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: To generate story content. Story preferences (theme, mood, age group) are sent to OpenAI's API. No personal information (names, emails) is sent.
  • RevenueCat: To manage subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase verification through Apple's servers.
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. We do not store credit card information.

6. Data Retention

Your account data and stories are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your story library

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email.
